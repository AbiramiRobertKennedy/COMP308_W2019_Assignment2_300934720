PROJECTS


BOOK HUB

Bookhub, is an application that provides a platform to search books of their interest through name, ISBN, image and even barcode, tracks their reading and stores them for future reference purposes. Also, suggests user with great books on the market which matches their interest. Users can chat about books to their friends often and can even provide reviews on the books they have read which can help other users as well.


SNOOKER CLUB

Worked in creating a webpage for Ozi’s Snooker Club. Created a page which can display professional player’s profiles and created database. Displays tournament calendar and also feedback options.


STORE LOCATOR

A well-developed online platform which directly connects donors and recipients and verifies charity organization authenticity, qualification as well as eligibility plays a critical role in letting people avoid fraud, donate wisely and receive safely and easily.
